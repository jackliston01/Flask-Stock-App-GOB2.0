About GOB 2.0

GOB: Green or Bust

GOB stands for "Green or Bust." Naturally, green is a direct reference to profit; however, green also means success. Irrespective of our environment, we, not only as individuals, but as a collective, strive for success which is not always dependent on stock success.

Our Stock Engine

GOB is a multifaceted stock engine that gathers utility and leaves behind unnecessary "fluff". From a ticker overview to a transcript AI summarizer, GOB centralizes one's research onto a single website, increasing efficiency for all.

Key Features

  • Ticker Overview

    Stock info all in one place:

    • Stock Price
    • General Data
    • Current News
    • Stock Chart
    • AI Evaluation

  • Transcript Analyzer and Summarizer

    Saves hours of reading monotonous and often unrealistic transcripts/court documents/research publications and more!

  • Rapid Development

    GOB has been structured to allow the development and deployment of new tools as quickly as possible.

Upcoming Features
